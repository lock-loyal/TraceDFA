Contest Fine, Allow citizen to contest a fine, Citizen. Press button to access online fine management, system redirects to desired section, press online fine payment button, system searches for unpaid fines in database, select fine to contest, system displays all available information and appeal form, enter necessary information, system verifies information correctness, forwards appeal request to relevant party, and notifies citizen, citizen must be authenticated in the system, this use case extends Payment Fine and is invoked when citizen clicks button related to contesting fines, or in case of generic errors system calls Service Unavailable use case.
