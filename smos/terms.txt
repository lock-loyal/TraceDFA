user: individuals in the system (students, teachers, administrators, parents).
classroom: physical or virtual space for instruction.
teaching: educational course or instructional session.
role: permission designation (e.g., student, teacher, administrator).
address: location details for users or classrooms.
report: summary document for records or analysis.
absence: non-attendance at an expected activity.
justify: documented reason for absence or delay.
register: attendance or enrollment tracking record.
note: comments or remarks about entities.
administrator: user role with administrative privileges.
student: user role focused on learning activities.
teacher: user role responsible for instruction.
votes: feedback or decisions from users.
statistics: numerical data insights from system records.
delay: tardiness to an activity.
