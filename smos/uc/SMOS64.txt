Name: ViewRegistriesList  
Actors: Directorate  
Description: Displays the list of registries for an academic year  
Preconditions:  
• The user must be logged into the system as Directorate  
• The user clicks the "Digital Registry" button  

**Sequence of Events**  
**User** | **System**  
--- | ---  
2. Selects the academic year | 1. Displays a screen for selecting the academic year of interest  
3. Searches the archive for all digital registries of that year and displays them associated with their respective class  

**Postconditions:**  
• The system is displaying the list of registries for a specific academic year  
• The Directorate interrupts the operation  
• Connection to the SMOS server is interrupted
