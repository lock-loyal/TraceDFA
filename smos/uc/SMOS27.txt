Name: ViewRegistersList  
Actors: Administrator  
Description: Displays the list of registers for an academic year  
Preconditions:  
• The user must be logged into the system as an Administrator  
• Click on the "Digital Register" button  

Event Sequence  
User  
System  
2. Select the academic year  
1. Display a screen for selecting the academic year of interest.  
3. Search the archive for all digital registers from that year and display them associated with their respective classes.  

Postconditions:  
• The system is displaying the list of registers related to a specific academic year  
• The administrator interrupts the operation  
• Connection to the SMOS server is interrupted
